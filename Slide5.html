Crisis Pregnancy Centers

Many women seeking reproductive health care encounter crisis pregnancy centers. While some of these centers provide actual health care, many of these centers strongly discourage patients from pursuing certain reproductive choices including engaging in sexual activity, using birth control, and undergoing abortion.
