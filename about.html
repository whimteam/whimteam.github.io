About WHIM

WHIM stands for Women's Health Information Maze. The game aims to raise awareness about the low accessibility of reproductive health care in the United States. WHIM is a maze, representing the maze of barriers women must navigate in order to obtain safe and reliable reproductive health care.

We believe that everyone, no matter their stance on controversial topics such as abortion and use of contraception, deserves the safe, affordable, and reliable sexual health care that they want.



WHIM was created using Blender. This website was created using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and is hosted on Github. CSS was adapted from Lavish.
